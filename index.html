Janushash Calculator

Janushash is Warthog's combined GPU+CPU hashing algorithm. It blends sha256t (GPU) and verushash2_2 (CPU) hashrates.

Formula: Janusscore = round(gpu * 10 * ((0.005 + cpu/gpu)^0.3 - 0.005^0.3) / 3), see here for reference.
